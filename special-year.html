The Golden Jubilee Celebration

Fifty years of devotion, culture, and community spirit, culminating in a celebration like never before.

Mohalaya Rally

To celebrate the sacred dawn of Devi Paksha, we will hold a Mahalaya Rally, inviting all devotees to join in chants, songs, and spiritual energy to welcome Maa Durga.

Mahalaya Prasad & Programs

After the rally, Prasad will be distributed. We will also host small cultural and devotional programs to mark the divine occasion and spread joy in the community.

Prasad Distribution (Two Days)

Special prasad will be served on two evenings: Ashtami Night (Khichidi Bhog) and Navami Night (Payash - Rice Kheer).

Daily Aarti (Shashti to Navami)

On all four puja days of udrga puja , Aarti will be performed with devotion, filling the atmosphere with chants, bells, conch shells and divine energy.

Ashtami Bhog

The most auspicious offering, Maha Ashtami Bhog, will be prepared, offered to Maa Durga, and then distributed among all devotees.

Navami Programs

Fun and devotional competitions including Arati Dance, Quiz on Durga Maa, Jokar competition, and the traditional Ullu Dhoni Competition.

Dashami Sindur Khela

On Bijoya Dashami, married women will celebrate the age-old ritual of Sindur Khela, symbolizing joy, power, and prosperity.
